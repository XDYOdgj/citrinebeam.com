iDOO Queen Air Mattress - The Perfect Choice for Comfort and Convenience

The iDOO Queen Air Mattress delivers unmatched comfort through its innovative four-chamber design, providing support similar to a traditional mattress. This modern air mattress is equipped with a built-in electric pump, making it easy to inflate and deflate in just seconds. No more dealing with manual valves or long inflation times with this smart, user-friendly option.

Its reinforced PVC material with K80 resin ensures durability, keeping the mattress firm and resilient even after multiple uses. The non-slip base enhances stability, prevents shifting during the night, and keeps things quiet and secure. Whether you're hosting guests at home or venturing into the great outdoors, this mattress is engineered for all occasions.

  • Four Chamber Design - Enhances support while ensuring even pressure distribution
  • Durable PVC Material with K80 Resin - Resists punctures and maintains firmness over time
  • Non-Slip Base - Keeps the mattress secure, quiet, and stable for restful sleep
