Therm-a-Rest Compressible Cinch Camping Pillow

Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience with the Therm-a-Rest Compressible Cinch Camping Pillow. Designed for adventurers who demand quality without compromise, this pillow is your go-to companion for restful sleep in the wild.

Featuring a smart cinch cord system, you can easily adjust the firmness and support to match your personal sleeping preferences. Whether you're hiking across rugged terrain or relaxing in a peaceful campsite, this pillow adapts to your needs, ensuring a comfortable and customizable sleep experience.

Constructed with a soft, 60% recycled polyester cover and upcycled sleeping pad foam stuffing, it's an excellent choice for eco-conscious travelers. This pillow not only supports your rest but also supports the planet, making every adventure more sustainable.

The ultra-lightweight insulation layer provides a smooth and even surface, reducing pressure points and offering a night of undisturbed sleep. Its compact and durable design makes it ideal for packing into your backpack or gear bag, without sacrificing comfort or quality.

Perfect for solo or family trips, this compressible travel pillow is designed to keep up with your outdoor lifestyle. Say goodbye to restless nights and hello to a reliable, eco-friendly sleep solution on every adventure.
