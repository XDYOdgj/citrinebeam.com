Gerber Baby Fleece Lined Wrap Fasten Non-Skid Soft Slipper Booties

These adorable baby booties are designed to keep your little one's feet cozy and safe throughout the day. Featuring a soft poly fleece interior lining, they offer extra warmth and comfort, while the non-skid cotton canvas sole provides stability and grip, making them perfect for playtime and early walking adventures.

With a secure hook & eye wrap fasten system, these booties stay firmly in place without slipping, and the heel loop ensures easy on and off, so you can help your baby get dressed quickly and effortlessly. The gentle design and supple material are kind to delicate skin, making them a reliable and stylish choice for every day use.

Available in a soft green color with a unique camo pattern, these booties blend functionality with a fashionable look. Ideal for the nursery, outdoor outings, or any activity that requires soft slipper comfort, they are a must-have for any baby's wardrobe.

Key Features

  • Soft poly fleece interior lining for comfort
  • Non-skid cotton canvas sole for safety
  • Hook & eye wrap fasten for secure fit
  • Heel loop for easy removal and fitting
  • Green bootie with camo pattern design

Why Choose These Booties?

Whether you're looking for stylish baby booties that double as soft slipper comfort, or safe footwear with a non-skid sole, these booties deliver. The fleece lined construction ensures your baby stays warm, while the wrap fasten system ensures a snug fit, and the heel loop adds convenience for easy wear and tear.

These booties are the perfect addition to a baby's outfit, combining comfort, style, and safety in one essential piece. Ideal for gentle exploration and warm weather, they will keep your baby's feet cozy and secure all day long.
